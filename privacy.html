Privacy policy

The short version: we collect the minimum needed to bake for you, and we never sell it.

Last updated July 10, 2026.

sasha's bakeshop ("we", "us", the one-person bakery in SE Portland) runs this website at sashasbakeshop.com. Here is what happens with your information, in plain language.

What we collect

When you order: checkout happens on Stripe's secure payment page. Stripe collects your name, email, phone number, payment details, fulfillment choice, delivery address (if you choose delivery), and any notes you add. We see everything except your full payment details, which stay with Stripe. We use this information to bake, confirm, and hand over your order, and for the bookkeeping the tax folks require.

When you join the newsletter or a notify-me list: we collect your email address and use it to send you menus, journal posts, and order announcements. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing always works.

When you use the contact form: the form opens your own email app. Nothing is stored on this website.

Just browsing: this site sets no cookies of its own and runs no ad trackers or analytics.

What we never do

We never sell your information, rent it, trade it, or share it with anyone for marketing. Your email is for bread news only.

Third parties involved

A few services help run this site, and each handles some data under its own privacy policy:

Stripe processes payments (stripe.com/privacy). GitHub Pages hosts the site and, like any web host, may keep standard server logs including IP addresses. Google Fonts serves one of our typefaces, which means your browser requests a file from Google when the page loads. Links to Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook take you to those platforms, where their rules apply.

Your choices

Want your information corrected or deleted, or curious what we have (it isn't much)? Email info@sashasbakeshop.com and we'll sort it out within a few days.

Changes

If this policy changes, the update appears on this page with a new date up top. Big changes get a mention in the newsletter.

Questions? Say hi any time.
