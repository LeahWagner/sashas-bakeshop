Tahini chocolate chunk

Vegan, fudgy, and gone first almost every week.

← Back to the menu

$4 each

Tahini does what butter does in most cookies, but better: it keeps the middle dense and fudgy and adds a toasty, nutty depth that makes the dark chocolate taste even darker. Big melty chunks (never chips), a swirl of sesame through the dough, and flaky salt on top.

Fully vegan, and nobody can ever tell. Bring one to your most skeptical friend.
