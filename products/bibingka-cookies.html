Bibingka cookies

The one that started the whole bakeshop.

← Back to the menu

$4.50 each

Bibingka is a Filipino coconut rice cake, traditionally baked in banana leaves until the edges char and the sugars toast. This cookie is my love letter to it: chewy coconut and rice flour, rich salted coconut milk, and a sprinkle of sugar that's been slow-toasted on real banana leaf until it tastes warm and a little smoky.

It's soft in the middle, crisp at the edge, and tastes like nothing else at the Saturday table. If it's your first order, start here.

Made with rice flour + coconut · contains dairy and egg · baked fresh Saturday morning
