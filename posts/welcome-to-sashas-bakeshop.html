Welcome to sasha's bakeshop

← Back to the journal

Hi. I'm Sasha, and this is the very first post from my very own bakeshop. Typing that sentence still feels a little unreal.

Here's the short version of how we got here: I have been obsessed with food for as long as I can remember. Not just eating it (though, yes), but the whole thing. Why a recipe works, why it doesn't, what happens if you toast the sugar first, whether the seventeenth version of a cookie can be better than the sixteenth. It can. I checked.

A few months ago I moved to Portland. New city, new kitchen, and honestly? I don't know a soul here yet. Opening a bakeshop when you know exactly zero people is either brave or ridiculous, and I've decided not to investigate which. What I do know is that food is how I make friends, and a warm cookie is a very good opening line.

So this is me, introducing myself to a whole city the only way I know how: small batches, baked in my licensed home kitchen, of the flavors I'm most excited about. Bibingka cookies with toasted banana leaf sugar. Black sesame rolls. Whatever I can't stop thinking about next week.

This journal is where I'll bring you along: the experiments, the wins, the flops (there will be flops, and they will be funny), and the first look at every weekly special. If you want it in your inbox, the newsletter signup is on the journal page.

I'm so happy you're here. Come say hi on Instagram, ask me anything, and tell me what you want to see in the box. And if you're hungry:
