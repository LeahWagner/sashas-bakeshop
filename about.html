About the bakeshop

One baker, one home kitchen, two dogs.

Hi, I'm Sasha

I've loved baking for as long as I can remember, and I spent a few years working in pastry before moving to Portland and finally opening a shop of my own. Sasha's Bakeshop is a licensed home microbakery in SE Portland, and everything is mixed, proofed, and baked in my kitchen, usually with two dogs stationed hopefully by the oven.

My favorite part of going somewhere new is ordering the thing I've never tried before. That's the fun I want to share here: bibingka cookies with toasted banana leaf sugar, black sesame milk rolls, coffee cake heavy with Oregon hazelnut streusel. Familiar enough to crave, different enough to surprise you.

Orders open every Thursday at 9am and everything is baked fresh Saturday morning. Specials rotate weekly, and the mainstays are always there.
